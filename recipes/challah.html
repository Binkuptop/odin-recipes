THE BEST JEWISH CHALLAH EVER

Home made very yummy good and not sweet challah that we have learned from our jewish guru. When we were out hiking in machu picho we met a rabbai, he left us with a secret message that we had to decipher. At the end we found out it actully was gibrih and he was cenile but his wife gave us this challah recipe.

ingredents

  • 2 tbsp yeast
  • 0.5 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup warmish water
  • 1 kilo flour
  • 2 tbsp fine salt
  • 0.5 cups white sugar
  • 0.25 cup canola oil
  • 1.5 cups warmish water

In a small bowel mix yeas, half a tsp sugar and 1 cup water and leav to buble. In a large bowl mix dry ingredents, create well and add all liquids. mix with spoon, kneed and let rest for 45 min. kneed for 10sec and let rest to rise for another 45min. seperate and bread your most amazing challahs. bake in 185c oven for 25-30 mins.
